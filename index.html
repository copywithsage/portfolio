04 — About

Before I wrote copy, I read people.

A copywriter who understood people before learning to write.

For three years before I started writing copy, I was on the other side of the counter. Managing a spa, handling customer service, talking to real people about real problems they wanted solved.

I watched what made someone lean in. What made them reach out. Which words on a price list made them book the treatment, and which ones made them say they'd "think about it" and never come back.

Copywriting came later. But the part most writers struggle with, understanding what the reader actually wants to hear, and why they're hesitating, I'd been practicing that for years.

That's what you get when you work with me. Not a copywriter guessing at your customer. A copywriter who's been the person they're trying to reach.
